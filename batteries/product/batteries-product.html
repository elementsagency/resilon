Resilon Conventional Car Battery 12V – 74Ah – 640CCA (OE Equivalent)

Description

Ensure reliable starting power with the Resilon 12V 74Ah Car Battery. Built to OE-equivalent standards, it delivers strong cold cranking performance (640CCA) and long-lasting durability, making it ideal for a wide range of UK vehicles.

- OE equivalent performance for seamless replacement

- Designed and tested for UK driving conditions

- Voltage: 12V
- Capacity: 74Ah
